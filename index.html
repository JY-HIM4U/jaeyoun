Jaeyoun Choi

I'm a Ph.D. student in at MIT collaborating with Professor Pulkit Agrawal. I believe robots have potential to drastically improve our daily lives. However, most current robots lack in adaptability and versatility. I believe that hardware with high fidelity and control based on Reinforcement Learning can solve this problem.

Before I joined MIT, I served as the Chief Technical Officer at Neumafit Corporation, where I focused on developing an AI-powered wearable mask for cardiorespiratory analysis. Prior to that, I completed my B.S. and M.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Seoul National University, where I conducted research at the Soft Robotics and Bionics Laboratory.
